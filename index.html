About Me

I'm currently a student at Southern New Hampshire University and enrolled in my capstone for a Bachelors in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering. During my time with SNHU, I have created a strong foundation in Computer Sciences by learning the basics of the Software Development Lifecycle and Team Development utilizing the AGILE methodology, various Operating Systems, all the way to Client/Server Development. Furthermore, with a concentration in Software Engineering, I have also learned about Emerging Systems Architecture and Technology, Secure Coding, and Software Reverse Engineering. This curriculum has helped to develop a new focus for program design, team development, and problem solving within the computer science field.
